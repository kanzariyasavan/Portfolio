About Me
01

Crafting elegant backends, one route at a time

I'm Savan Kanzariya — a Laravel developer who takes pride in writing clean, well-structured PHP that scales gracefully.

Over 1.5+ years of professional development, I've worked across the full web stack — building robust REST APIs consumed by mobile apps, crafting feature-rich admin dashboards with Filament and Livewire, and designing normalized database schemas that perform under load.

I follow Laravel best practices: service-repository patterns, Eloquent relationships, job queues with Horizon, automated testing with PHPUnit, and clean API resources with proper versioning.

Beyond Laravel, I'm comfortable with Vue.js for front-end interactivity, writing migrations and seeders, setting up CI/CD pipelines, and deploying on shared hosting or VPS environments.
